President's Message

       Occidental Mindoro State College is a state institution of higher learning that shall be the driving force in uplifting human living conditions and improving the quality of life of the people in the province of Occidental Mindoro and the country. It shall provide affordable and quality education and shall offer innovative solutions to various societal challenges through its instruction, relevant research and extension efforts.

Elbert C. Edaniol
SUC President III

Why Choose OMSC?

Just before the year 2021 ended, Occidental Mindoro State College welcomed a new administration with its 5th President, the youngest administrator of the College, Dr. Elbert C. Edaniol, by virtue of Board of Trustees Resolution No. 31, series of 2021.

Vision

A premier higher education institution that develops globally competitive, locally responsive, innovative professionals, and life-long learners.

Mission

OMSC is committed to produce intellectual and human capital by developing excellent graduates through outcomes-based instruction, relevant research, responsive technical advisory services, community engagement, and sustainable production.

Goals

  1. To continually increase access to quality education;

Alumni Corner

Our Most Valuable Alumni

Mary Queen Tan Bernardo

OMSC Alumni Trustee

Miss Mary Queen Tan Bernardo is the first-ever alumni trustee in history and marks a significant milestone, as it signifies the recognition of alumni's invaluable contributions and expertise, ensuring their direct involvement in shaping the future policies and decisions of the institution. This groundbreaking initiative fosters a strong sense of alumni engagement, creating a bridge between past and present students, and promoting a vibrant and enduring community within the institution.

Hon. Mark O. Go

Chair of the Lower House Committee on Higher, Technical, and Vocational Education

Hon. Mark O. Go who graduated from our esteemed institution is a testament to our commitment to fostering leadership and public service. Their education here equipped them with the skills and knowledge needed to make impactful changes, exemplifying the excellence of our educational programs.

Miguel T. Guevarra

Cybersecurity Specialist

Mr. Miguel T. Guevarra was the first ever Information Technology Almuni of our esteemed institution who was able to predict the attacks of ransomwares that managed to infiltrate other Government Agencies and create a specialized software to protect OMSC's presence in cyberspace.
